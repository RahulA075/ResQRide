## roadside-assistance-bg.jpg
Save the roadside assistance image (showing a person with a broken down vehicle and a technician in orange) as `roadside-assistance-bg.jpg` in this directory.

The image should show:
- A person in casual clothes standing next to a broken down vehicle
- A roadside assistance technician in orange high-visibility clothing
- The scene should convey professional roadside assistance service

This image is used as the background for the login screen.
